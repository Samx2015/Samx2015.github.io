Before Contacting Support

  • Make sure MacHelper is launched from Applications, not directly from a temporary download location.
  • If Finder right-click menus do not appear, open MacHelper's menu and choose Configure Finder right-click menus.
  • If a Finder action cannot open a selected folder, macOS may need you to approve that access prompt.
  • If the menu-bar item is missing, open macOS Control Center settings and check Menu Bar items.

Finder Right-Click Menus

MacHelper uses a bundled Finder extension. macOS controls whether that extension is enabled. You can open the permission/settings flow from the MacHelper menu, or open System Settings and look under Login Items & Extensions.

Feedback

Use Report a Bug, Suggest a Feature, or Send Feedback from the MacHelper menu. MacHelper opens an email draft so you can review everything before sending it.

Contact

Email admin@xintechllc.com. Please include your macOS version, Mac model, and a short description of what you expected to happen.
