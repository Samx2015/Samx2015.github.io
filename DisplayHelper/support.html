Contact

For support, privacy questions, bugs, or ideas, email admin@xintechllc.com.

You can also use the feedback items in the app menu. They open an email draft so you can review everything before sending it.

Helpful Details

When reporting an issue, please include:

  • What happened and what you expected to happen.
  • Your macOS version and Mac model, if known.
  • Whether the MacBook screen was mirrored or extended.
  • The steps that reproduce the issue.

Bug report drafts may include a short diagnostic note. Remove anything you do not want to send.

Common Notes

  • DisplayHelper appears in the macOS menu bar and does not show a Dock icon.
  • The App Store build is made for mirrored display setups.
  • No account, analytics, ads, or tracking are included.
