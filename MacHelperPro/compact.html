What is it?

A Mac utility suite.

  • Black out the built-in display darkens the MacBook screen when it mirrors an external monitor
  • Keep useful controls close mirror, dim, sleep, display levels, keyboard brightness, shortcuts, Wi-Fi switching, Finder actions, Safes, Secure Pocket, and optional Full Disk Access guidance live close by
  • Encrypt and pocket local files create .safe archives from Finder selections or keep small private items in Secure Pocket
  • 100% confidential unlike Google Drive, OneDrive, GitHub, and other general-purpose cloud or code services, Secure Pocket keeps plaintext, passwords, and encryption keys on your Mac
  • Stay out of the way no Dock window, no account, no analytics, and clear guidance for optional Full Disk Access

Less fuss at your desk.

  • Helpful, not noisy runs from the menu bar without a Dock window, account, or analytics
