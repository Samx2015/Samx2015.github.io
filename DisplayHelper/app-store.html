DisplayHelper

The easiest way to install and update DisplayHelper.

What it is for

For mirrored Mac setups where the external monitor is the screen you want to watch.

  • Darken the built-in screen.
  • Mirror or unmirror from the menu.
  • Dim displays when stepping away.
  • Adjust brightness and contrast.

Privacy

No account, analytics, tracking, ads, or automatic reports.

  • Display controls run locally.
  • Feedback opens an email draft.
  • You can remove logs before sending.

Menu controls

Everything stays in the menu bar.

Enable auto blackout, mirror, dim, adjust brightness and contrast, sleep, send feedback, and open privacy controls from one menu.
