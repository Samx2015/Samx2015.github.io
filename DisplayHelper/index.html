DisplayHelper

Keep the MacBook screen out of the way while your external display stays in focus.

View App Store Version

Made for desks, demos, classrooms, and presentations where the MacBook is nearby, but the external display is the screen you want to watch.

What it does

Simple display controls from the menu bar.

DisplayHelper can darken the built-in display while mirroring, temporarily dim active displays until the next key press, or put your Mac to sleep when you are done.

Mirroring

The external display is the one people watch, so DisplayHelper keeps the built-in screen dark.

Extended

The MacBook screen is part of your workspace, so DisplayHelper leaves it alone.

Away

Dim the screens temporarily, then restore them with the next key press when you return.

Menu at a glance

One small menu for the tasks above.

  • ✓Enable Auto Blackout for mirrored setups.
  • ✓Mirror or unmirror the built-in display when your setup changes.
  • ✓Dim, sleep, and send feedback without leaving the menu bar.

Privacy

Local by design.

No account is required, and the app does not include analytics, tracking, ads, or automatic reports. Feedback opens a draft in your email app, so nothing is sent unless you choose to send it.
