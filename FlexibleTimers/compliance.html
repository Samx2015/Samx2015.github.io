Flexible Timers Compliance

Flexible Timers is a macOS timer app for creating timers, countdowns, clocks, and account-based reminder workflows.

Flexible Timers is operated by Xintech LLC.

Support

For support, see xintechllc.com/FlexibleTimers/support.html.

Messaging Compliance

Flexible Timers SMS is limited to account phone verification codes and user-created timer/reminder SMS sent only to a signed-in user's own verified, opted-in account phone number. Third-party recipient messaging is not enabled. SMS is not a two-way chat or conversation service.

SMS keywords: STOP opts out, HELP returns support information, and START or YES opts a previously opted-out phone number back in after app consent. These inbound keywords are used only for compliance and account-phone messaging controls. Response message examples are published in the SMS Terms and opt-in evidence pages.
