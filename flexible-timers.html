Flexible Timers

Flexible Timers is a macOS app for timers, countdowns, clocks, and reminder workflows.

It is designed for people who keep several time-based tasks running at once: focused work sessions, breaks, meetings, cooking, errands, world clocks, and longer reminders that should stay visible without taking over the desktop.

What it does

  • Create regular timers for activities that count up from the moment they start.
  • Create countdown tasks with a fixed duration or a specific end time.
  • Keep multiple timers visible in a compact single-column or double-column window.
  • Track world clocks for different cities alongside personal timers.
  • Add names, descriptions, reminder patterns, and sounds to timer tasks.
  • Send reports and email reminders to your own account email address.
  • Use the app as a lightweight desktop timing dashboard for daily routines.

Why it is useful

Flexible Timers is built around quick scanning. Each row shows the timer value, label, type, and status controls in a small layout that works well as an always nearby utility window. It is useful when one timer is not enough and a calendar app would be too heavy for the job.
